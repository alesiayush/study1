# How are these files made

- fridayThe13thNight.json is a result of http://ggrna.dbcls.jp/hs/thirteen+fri.json
- merge-test-c.vcf is from examples/ of vcftools v0.1.13
- test_input_1_a.[s|b]am are from test/dat/ of samtools v1.5 
- tommo_EGFR.txt tommo_EGFR.txt is made by 'grep EGFR' command against https://humandbs.biosciencedbc.jp/files/hum0015/hum0015.v1.freq.v1.zip
- linda.txt refers https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Linda_(genus)
- GTH.tar.gz is made by emihat
